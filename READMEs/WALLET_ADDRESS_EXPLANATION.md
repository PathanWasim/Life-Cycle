## 🔍 How to Identify Your Real Address

**Real wallet address characteristics:**
- Starts with `0x`
- Followed by exactly 40 hexadecimal characters (0-9, a-f, A-F)
- Unique to YOUR MetaMask wallet
- Shown in MetaMask when you click your account name

**Example addresses (NOT yours):**
- `0x1234567890abcdef1234567890abcdef12345678` ← Too simple, placeholder
- `0xAbC1234567890dEf1234567890AbC1234567890` ← Mixed case pattern, placeholder

## 📝 Steps to Get Your Real Address

### Step 1: Open MetaMask
- Click the MetaMask fox icon in your browser toolbar
- Enter your password if locked

### Step 2: Check Network
- Look at the top left corner
- Should say "Polygon Amoy Testnet"
- If not, click the dropdown and select it

### Step 3: Copy Your Address
- Click on "Account 1" (or your account name) at the top
- Your address will appear below
- Click on it to copy
- It will look like: `0x7a2B...c4D9` (shortened view)

### Step 4: Verify Full Address
- After copying, paste it somewhere to see the full address
- Should be 42 characters total (0x + 40 hex characters)


## 💡 What is a Wallet Address?

Think of it like:
- **Bank Account Number**: Your unique identifier on the blockchain
- **Email Address**: Others can send you cryptocurrency using this address
- **Public Information**: Safe to share (unlike private key!)

## 🔐 Private Key vs Wallet Address

| Wallet Address | Private Key |
|----------------|-------------|
| Public - Safe to share | Private - NEVER share |
| Like email address | Like password |
| Receive cryptocurrency | Control/spend cryptocurrency |
| 42 characters (0x + 40) | 64 characters (no 0x) |
| Example: 0x742d35Cc... | Example: abc123def456... |
